Psychological Techniques Employed

  • Sense of Urgency — A 12-hour deadline was imposed to pressure immediate action without careful consideration.
  • Appeal to Authority — The email impersonated the official UM IT Helpdesk to establish credibility.
  • Fear of Loss — The threat of losing email access created anxiety to override critical thinking.

Indicators of Phishing (Red Flags)

  • Suspicious URL: Hovering over the button would reveal a non-official university link.
  • Unusual Request: Legitimate IT departments do not request account validation via email links.

Recommended Security Practices

  • Always verify the sender's email domain before taking action.
  • Hover over hyperlinks to inspect URLs before clicking.
  • Do not act hastily on urgent requests — verify through official channels.
  • Contact PTM directly using official university contact information when uncertain.
